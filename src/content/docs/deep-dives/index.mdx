---
title: Phase 3 · Deep-Dive Case Library
description: Future comparative studies organized by economic archetype.
---

These studies will be migrated and refined incrementally. Each will compare companies
that look similar on the surface but create value through different mechanisms.

| Case family | Core mechanism | Questions to answer |
|---|---|---|
| Payments and vertical operating systems | Volume, take rate, workflow lock-in | Who owns the customer? What is true owner cash? |
| Networks, advertising, and software | Data, engagement, switching, SBC | Who captures rent as agents automate decisions? |
| Semiconductors and cyclicals | Capacity, yield, scarcity, mid-cycle earnings | Is profit structural or cyclical? |
| Allocators and conglomerates | Float, SOTP, internal capital markets | Does management turn retained cash into per-share value? |
| Physical builders and housing | Inventory, land, utilization, leverage | Which assets are optional and which are liabilities? |
| Consumer and experience platforms | Brand, repeat demand, trust, distribution | Is emotional value durable and economically captured? |
