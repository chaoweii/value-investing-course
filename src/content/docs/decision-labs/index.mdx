title: Opening Decision Labs
description: Commit your first judgments before learning the framework.
---

Before opening a case, create a dated entry in its Markdown journal. Your first
answer is not supposed to be correct. It is supposed to be honest.

## Required decision

For each company, record:

- Buy, hold, monitor, or pass.
- Confidence from 1 to 5.
- Maximum position size.
- Why the market might be wrong.
- The three assumptions carrying most of the valuation.
- Evidence that would falsify the thesis.
