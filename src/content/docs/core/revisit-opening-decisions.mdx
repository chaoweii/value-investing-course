---
title: Revisit The Opening Decisions
description: Compare your initial judgments with your owner-underwriting conclusions.
---

After completing Phases 1 and 2, reopen the three Markdown journals.

For each company, write:

1. The most important belief you changed.
2. The assumption that now carries the valuation.
3. The strongest argument against your current conclusion.
4. The evidence needed before changing position size.
5. Whether the stock clears BRK.B and the S&P 500 after adjusting for risk.

The goal is not to prove that your first answer was wrong. The goal is to make the
reasoning behind your final answer explicit, falsifiable, and repeatable.
