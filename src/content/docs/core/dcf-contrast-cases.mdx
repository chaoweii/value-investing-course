## Adyen: same logic, additional translation layers

Adyen requires segment growth, merchant-adjusted cash, EUR operating forecasts, EUR/USD
translation, and the `100 ADS = 1 ordinary share` bridge. Management guidance constrains
near-term growth and margin assumptions, but does not eliminate execution risk.

## Berkshire: model selection is part of valuation

Berkshire has a yearly DCF output but no canonical dependency-aware operating model in the
current workbench. That is a warning, not a missing cosmetic feature. Float, insurance,
operating subsidiaries, securities, and liquidity often make SOTP and per-share intrinsic-value
growth more informative than forcing one consolidated FCF path.

| Case | Operating model available | Primary teaching warning |
|---|---:|---|
| TOST | {String(dcf.companies.TOST.operatingModelAvailable)} | Do not let FCF outgrow operating drivers or ignore SBC. |

| BRK.B | {String(dcf.companies['BRK.B'].operatingModelAvailable)} | Do not force a single DCF onto allocator economics. |
