## Margins are consequences

Forecast cost of revenue, sales and marketing, R&D, G&A, capex, working capital, and leases
independently. A cost line may scale more slowly than revenue only when a causal mechanism exists:
automation, fixed infrastructure, mature sales cohorts, better mix, or pricing.

Margin expansion becomes fragile when it requires:

- service quality to remain unchanged despite fewer resources;
- customer acquisition costs to fall without evidence;
- capex or working capital to disappear;
- every business segment to approach the best segment’s margin;
- reinvestment to fall while high growth continues.
