## The complete bridge

```text
Customer and unit economics
→ revenue − operating costs − reinvestment
→ owner FCF
→ present value of operating cash flows
= enterprise value
+ underwriteable owner cash − debt and other claims
= equity value
÷ diluted shares
= value per share
```

Accounting earnings measure economic activity under accounting rules. Reported FCF measures a
period’s cash movement. Owner FCF asks how much cash can belong to shareholders without weakening
the business. DCF values that future owner cash today.

<ChineseNote title="先问你在估值什么">
企业价值属于所有资本提供者；股权价值属于股东；每股价值还要经过稀释股数。把三者混在一起，是 DCF 最常见的基础错误。
</ChineseNote>
