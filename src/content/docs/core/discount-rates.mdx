## Two useful questions

**WACC asks:** what return does the market require from the company’s capital providers?

**Personal hurdle asks:** what return must this uncertain investment offer versus Treasury bills,
the S&P 500, BRK.B, and your best alternatives?

Use WACC to estimate market-consistent operating value. Use a required-return hurdle to decide
whether the resulting opportunity deserves your capital. Do not add an arbitrary discount-rate
penalty for every uncertainty already modeled in the cash-flow scenarios.

```text
Required equity return ≈ Treasury yield + equity-risk premium + company-specific risk judgment
```
