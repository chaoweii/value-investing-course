## Growth must have a physical explanation

For Toast:

```text
Locations × GPV per location × payments economics
+ locations × subscription attachment and price
+ adjacent fintech and services
= recurring economic revenue
```

For Adyen:

```text
Merchant volume × net revenue yield
+ wallet-share expansion
+ Unified Commerce and Platforms adoption
+ financial-product attachment
= net revenue
```

TAM constrains what is possible. It does not prove market share, customer acquisition, retention,
pricing, or profitability. Always compare percentage growth with absolute additions: a declining
growth rate can still produce larger annual revenue additions.
