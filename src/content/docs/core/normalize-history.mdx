## Do not forecast a noisy quarter

Reconcile at least three years of revenue, operating income, operating cash flow, capex, working
capital, SBC, leases, taxes, and unusual items. Then explain every normalization.

| Item | Question |
|---|---|
| Working capital | Timing noise or structural cash need? |
| Capex | Maintenance, growth, or temporarily deferred? |
| SBC | Real recurring compensation or unusual award? |
| Restructuring | Truly one-time, or repeated operating cost? |
| Customer funds | Owner cash, or offset by an obligation? |

TOST’s memory-chip inventory timing may depress a period’s reported FCF, but that does not permit
blindly adding it back forever. ADYEY’s merchant balances are real cash but not automatically
distributable owner cash.

<ChineseNote title="正常化不是美化">
正常化的目的，是找出可持续经济能力；不是把所有坏消息都标成“一次性”，把所有好消息都年化。
</ChineseNote>
