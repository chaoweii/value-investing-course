A customer can love a product while common shareholders receive little value. The investor must
trace the product through every economic layer:

<ToastCaseMap />

Toast is useful because the operating mechanism is tangible. A restaurant may depend on payments,
orders, menus, staff workflows, guest data, reservations, and reporting throughout every service.
That can create retention and module expansion. But Toast must still acquire the restaurant, install
hardware, support it reliably, fund product development, compensate employees, and compete on price.

## What crosses the inflection point?

An **inflection point** is a change in the direction or rate of an economic trend. A profitability
inflection occurs when each period's additional gross profit begins to exceed its additional operating
cost. It is not simply the year in which revenue becomes large.

```text
Additional economic gross profit
− additional sales, support, R&D, and administration
= incremental operating profit
```

If that result remains positive, operating profit can grow much faster than revenue. This is
**operating leverage**. The mechanism may come from a shared software platform, a maturing merchant
base, better module attachment, more productive sales cohorts, or fixed corporate infrastructure.

<ChineseNote title="拐点不是收入达到某个神奇数字">
真正的拐点是新增毛利开始持续大于新增成本。若算力、客服、获客或资本投入与收入一样快地增长，收入再大也不一定产生股东利润。
</ChineseNote>

## Toast's historical transition

The course's read-only workbench snapshot shows reported Toast operating income moving from
approximately **−$384 million in FY2022** to **+$16 million in FY2024** and **+$292 million in
FY2025**. That is evidence of an accounting-profit inflection. It does not finish the analysis.

The next questions are:

1. Did recurring gross profit grow because restaurants stayed, processed more volume, and adopted
   more modules?
2. Did operating costs grow more slowly without harming implementation or support?
3. Was cash flow helped by temporary working-capital or inventory timing?
4. How much reported cash flow is required to neutralize SBC dilution?
5. Can the same mechanism survive weaker restaurant spending or slower location additions?

## OpenAI as a contrast, not a conclusion

For a private AI platform, the plausible mechanism is similar but the evidence is weaker:

```text
Enterprise and consumer demand
× revenue retained after distribution
− inference and service cost
= contribution profit
− training, research, sales, safety, and corporate cost
− required infrastructure investment
= owner economics
```

Improving inference economics and rapid revenue growth could create substantial operating leverage.
But compute intensity, price competition, training expense, infrastructure commitments, SBC, and
governance may postpone or absorb it. Without audited public statements, “the inflection has arrived”
is an underwriting hypothesis, not a reported fact.

## The four-gate decision

| Gate | Question | Evidence required |
|---|---|---|
| Product | Is it genuinely valuable? | Merchant workflow and reference checks |
| Business | Does Toast retain durable economics? | Retention, gross-profit growth, support and acquisition efficiency |
| Share | Do economics compound per diluted share? | Owner FCF, SBC, buybacks, cash, and share count |
| Investment | Is the offered price attractive? | Bear/base/bull value and benchmark-relative return |
