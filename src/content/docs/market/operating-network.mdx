## Different actors solve different problems

| Actor | Primary job | What it does not guarantee |
|---|---|---|
| Broker | Routes orders and maintains customer accounts | Best future investment result |
| Exchange | Matches orders under published rules | A seller at your preferred price |
| Market maker | Quotes prices and manages inventory | Unlimited inventory or a fixed spread |
| Clearinghouse | Manages obligations between trading firms | Protection from investment loss |
| Custodian | Safekeeps assets and records ownership | Direct control of the issuer |
| Index provider | Defines membership and weights | Trading or valuation judgment |

Settlement completes the exchange of cash and securities after execution. A trade can be valid
economically yet create operational risk if a participant cannot deliver cash, collateral, or
shares on time.
