## Value and price are connected by a market

Fundamentals determine what a claim may be worth over time. Market structure determines which
buyer and seller set today's price. The two can diverge without either side being irrational.

> Index funds must obtain exposure, but they cannot force anyone to sell at the old price.

<MarketPlumbingMap />

<ChineseNote title="指数基金必须买，但卖家不必按旧价格卖">
指数基金受到规则约束，需要获得目标敞口。卖家没有同样的义务。如果供给不足，价格会上升、成交会减少，基金也可能暂时使用衍生品或承受跟踪误差。
