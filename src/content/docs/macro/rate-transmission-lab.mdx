## Your decision

Assume the 10-year Treasury rises by 100 bps and remains elevated for three years. Decide which
of the four companies deserves the largest valuation revision, which deserves the largest
operating revision, and which might partially benefit.

## Company-by-company transmission

| Company | Operating channel | Valuation channel | First conclusion to test |
|---|---|---|---|
| TOST | Restaurant formation, merchant health, financing, and consumer spend | High duration while owner FCF per share is still emerging | Multiple pressure can arrive before operating weakness |
| ADYEY | Enterprise commerce volumes, FX, customer investment, and cash yields | High duration because much of the thesis rests on long reinvestment | A debt-light compounder can still be rate-sensitive |
| BRK.B | Higher cash income; mixed effects across insurance, rail, energy, housing, and equities | Opportunity cost rises, but near-term earnings may benefit | It can become relatively more attractive without being immune |
| LEN | Mortgage affordability, incentives, deliveries, ASP, and land decisions | Lower multiples often accompany housing-cycle fear | This is primarily an operating shock, then a valuation shock |
| Used housing | Existing owners with low mortgages may not sell | Appraisal values can lag transaction weakness | High rates can freeze volume before prices clear |

<ChineseNote title="先拆原因，再改模型">
同样是十年期利率上升100个基点：如果来自强劲增长，TOST 与 ADYEY 的收入可能仍健康；如果来自通胀和财政风险，利润率、需求与估值可能同时承压。LEN 对抵押贷款利率的传导最直接。
</ChineseNote>

## Record the decision before revealing the rubric

1. Rank the four by **valuation-duration sensitivity**.
2. Rank them by **operating sensitivity**.
3. Identify one beneficiary and explain why the benefit might be temporary.
4. State the one macro observation that would falsify your interpretation.

<ExerciseReveal prompt="What is the disciplined answer structure?">
Separate cause, operating transmission, valuation transmission, adaptation, and price. ADYEY and
TOST usually rank high on valuation duration; LEN ranks highest on direct operating sensitivity;
BRK.B can benefit through cash income but still faces opportunity-cost and asset-repricing effects.
The rankings can change when the cause of the yield move changes.
</ExerciseReveal>
