## The learning loop

1. **Decide before theory.** Record what you believe and what would falsify it.
2. **Learn the owner framework.** Rebuild earnings, moat, reinvestment, and valuation from first principles.
3. **Understand the price of money.** Learn how Treasury yields alter businesses, valuations, and opportunity cost.
4. **Return to the same decision.** Identify exactly why your judgment changed—or why it did not.
5. **Repeat across business archetypes.** Learn which tools transfer and which do not.

## The bridge most investors skip

Business quality determines the cash flows you may receive. The price of money determines what
those uncertain future cash flows are worth today. The [Macro Backbone](/macro/) teaches this
connection without turning value investing into short-term rate forecasting.
