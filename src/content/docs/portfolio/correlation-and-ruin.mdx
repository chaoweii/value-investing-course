## Count failure mechanisms, not ticker symbols

TOST, ADYEY, FOUR, PYPL, and TTD appear to serve different customers. They can still share
valuation-duration, technology-spending, liquidity, or risk-appetite exposure. PDD, FUTU, JD,
and TCOM can share a China-policy shock even when their operating models differ.

Correlation is often lowest when it is least needed and highest during stress.

## Hidden-concentration audit

For every position, tag:

- geography and policy regime;
- customer demand cycle;
- financing and interest-rate sensitivity;
- valuation duration;
- commodity, capacity, or supply-chain dependency;
- governance and shareholder-rights risk;
- common competitors and technology transitions.

Then calculate the portfolio loss if each theme moves to its bear case simultaneously.
