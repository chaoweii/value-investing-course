## Long-term holding is active patience

The portfolio is designed for long ownership periods. Tax-free status means a mistake can be
corrected without tax friction, but it does not make turnover intelligent.

| Event | Default response |
|---|---|
| Ordinary price fluctuation | No action |
| Price crosses bear/base/bull valuation band | Mandatory review; no automatic trade |
| Earnings confirm the thesis | Update evidence and forward return; resize only if justified |
| Thesis weakens or breaks | Reduce or exit promptly |
| Weight breaches risk limit | Review concentration and correlated loss |
| Superior opportunity emerges | Compare forward asymmetry and evidence quality |

## Review cadence

- **Quarterly:** review operating evidence, capital allocation, falsifiers, and valuation.
- **Annually:** rebuild the portfolio allocation from current forward opportunities.
- **Immediately:** respond to thesis-breaking evidence or a material governance event.
- **Never automatically:** trade because a chart moved or a stop-loss fired.

<ChineseNote title="价格是信号，不是命令">
价格变化可以改变预期收益，也可以提醒你可能漏掉了信息。但只有重新验证企业、估值和组合风险之后，价格才可能成为交易理由。
</ChineseNote>

<ExerciseReveal prompt="A high-quality holding rises through your base value with no new operating information. What happens next?">
Open a mandatory valuation review. Recalculate forward expected return, opportunity cost, and
concentration. Do not automatically trim merely because the stock rose.
</ExerciseReveal>
