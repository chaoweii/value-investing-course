## The portfolio is a collection of business ownerships

The objective is not to win every decision. It is to own businesses at prices where favorable
outcomes matter more than mistakes, while ensuring that no mistake or shared failure mechanism
can permanently remove your ability to compound.

## Decision hierarchy

1. New operating evidence or a thesis change can justify resizing immediately.
2. Price changes alter forward expected return, not business conviction.
3. A valuation-band crossing triggers mandatory review, never an automatic trade.
4. Winners are not trimmed merely because they appreciated.
5. Losers are not averaged down unless intrinsic value is independently reaffirmed.
6. Tax-free status removes friction from correcting mistakes; it does not reward activity.
