## Build for survival and contribution

The lab begins with a deliberately conservative teaching portfolio. Adjust weights, examine
hidden themes, compare independent outcomes with correlated stress, and generate a durable
decision record.

<ChineseNote title="实验规则">
这里的概率与目标价都是分析假设，不是事实。实验的价值在于暴露组合对错误假设的敏感度，而不是产生自动交易建议。
</ChineseNote>

## Finish the capstone

1. Keep the portfolio within its permanent-loss and theme budgets.
2. Explain why every position deserves its weight.
3. Identify which winner could materially affect the portfolio.
4. State which shared shock worries you most.
5. Generate the Markdown decision record and complete `journals/long-term-portfolio-policy.md`.
